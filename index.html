National Parks of Kenya

National Parks Map

Park Highlights

  • Maasai Mara National Reserve - Known for the Great Migration and exceptional wildlife viewing.
  • Amboseli National Park - Famous for its large elephant herds and views of Mount Kilimanjaro.
  • Tsavo National Park - Offers diverse landscapes and a large population of wildlife.
  • Nairobi National Park - A unique park near the capital with a variety of species.
  • Samburu National Reserve - Home to rare wildlife and beautiful landscapes.
  • Aberdare National Park - Features dense forests and diverse fauna.

Wildlife in Kenya

Kenya's national parks are home to a variety of wildlife, including:

  • Elephants
  • Lions
  • Rhinos
  • Giraffes
  • Zebras
  • Hippos

Visitor Information

For visitors planning a trip to Kenya's national parks, consider the following tips:

  • Check park regulations and entry fees.
  • Bring appropriate clothing and gear for safaris.
  • Respect wildlife and follow park guidelines.
  • Plan your visit during the dry season for better wildlife sightings.
  • Book accommodations in advance, especially during peak seasons.
